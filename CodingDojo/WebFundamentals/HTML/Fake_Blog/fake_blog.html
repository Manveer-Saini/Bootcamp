Adam Ondra

Who am I? Well my name is Adam Ondra, I was born in the Czech Republic to two climbing parents. My parents loved both climbing and I so much that they took me to the crag with them for climbing. I started to climb at age 6 and set the world record for climbing 9a at age 13. I knew when I first climbed I wanted to be the best in the world. Eventually I went on to establish the grade 9b+ and 9c.
